This Auto-Pédestre explores the surroundings of Baschleiden in the Éislek, Luxembourg's hilly north of quiet forests and deeply cut river valleys. The route measures 7.2 km — about 2 hours of walking, depending on pace. The route gathers about 130 m of ascent, and most of the way follows forest and field tracks.

Along the way you pass Tempelskamp. The circuit carries the blue Auto-Pédestre waymark throughout; being a loop, it has no fixed start.
