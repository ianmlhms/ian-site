Around Wellenstein, vineyards and views over the Moselle valley set the scene — the loop links vine slopes, plateau tracks and quiet side valleys. At 8 km, the circuit takes roughly 2½ hours. The route gathers about 180 m of ascent, and most of the way follows forest and field tracks.

On the route: Kuebendaellchen. The circuit carries the blue Auto-Pédestre waymark throughout; being a loop, it has no fixed start.
