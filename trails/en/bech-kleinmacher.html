Bech-Kleinmacher lies in the Moselle region in the country's south-east; the loop alternates between vines, fields and short stretches of woodland. The route measures 5.5 km — about 1½ hours of walking, depending on pace. The route gathers about 140 m of ascent, and most of the way follows forest and field tracks.

On the route: A Possen. The circuit carries the blue Auto-Pédestre waymark throughout; being a loop, it has no fixed start.
