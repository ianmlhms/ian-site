This Auto-Pédestre explores the surroundings of Contern in the Guttland, the varied heart of the country between fields and woods. The route measures 11.5 km — about 3 hours of walking, depending on pace. The route gathers about 180 m of ascent, mixing quiet lanes with field and forest paths.

On small paths, the loop links the prettiest corners around the village. Like every Auto-Pédestre, the loop is permanently waymarked with the blue symbol and can be started at any point.
