Around Clemency, in central Luxembourg, you cross the Guttland: gentle hills, fields, stream valleys and stretches of forest. At 12.1 km, the circuit takes roughly 3 hours. There is barely any climbing, and most of the way follows forest and field tracks.

The route mostly follows near-natural paths through woods and open country. The circuit carries the blue Auto-Pédestre waymark throughout; being a loop, it has no fixed start.
