Around Bergem, in central Luxembourg, you cross the Guttland: gentle hills, fields, stream valleys and stretches of forest. The route measures 8.1 km — about 2 hours of walking, depending on pace. There is barely any climbing, with about half the distance away from tarmac.

Field and forest tracks alternate with quiet back roads. The circuit carries the blue Auto-Pédestre waymark throughout; being a loop, it has no fixed start.
