This Auto-Pédestre explores the surroundings of Oetrange in the Guttland, the varied heart of the country between fields and woods. The loop is 11.6 km long; at a normal pace expect about 3 hours. There is barely any climbing, with about half the distance away from tarmac.

The route mostly follows near-natural paths through woods and open country. Like every Auto-Pédestre, the loop is permanently waymarked with the blue symbol and can be started at any point.
