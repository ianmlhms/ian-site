Limpertsberg lies in the Guttland — quiet farm tracks, woodland passages and village edges give this loop its rhythm. The loop is 7.5 km long; at a normal pace expect about 2 hours. There is barely any climbing, mixing quiet lanes with field and forest paths.

Along the way you pass Fort Marie and Fort Royal, among other sights. Like every Auto-Pédestre, the loop is permanently waymarked with the blue symbol and can be started at any point.
