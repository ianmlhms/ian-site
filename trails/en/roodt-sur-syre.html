This Auto-Pédestre explores the surroundings of Roodt sur Syre in the Guttland, the varied heart of the country between fields and woods. The loop is 10.8 km long; at a normal pace expect about 3 hours. There is barely any climbing, and most of the way follows forest and field tracks.

The route mostly follows near-natural paths through woods and open country. Like every Auto-Pédestre, the loop is permanently waymarked with the blue symbol and can be started at any point.
