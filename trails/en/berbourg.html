This Auto-Pédestre explores the area around Berbourg in the Mullerthal region, where sandstone, forest and small streams shape the landscape. The route measures 8.2 km — about 2 hours of walking, depending on pace. There is barely any climbing, with about half the distance away from tarmac.

Along the way you pass Burgruine Berbourg. The circuit carries the blue Auto-Pédestre waymark throughout; being a loop, it has no fixed start.
