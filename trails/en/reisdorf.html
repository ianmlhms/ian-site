This Auto-Pédestre explores the surroundings of Reisdorf in the Éislek, Luxembourg's hilly north of quiet forests and deeply cut river valleys. The route measures 9 km — about 3 hours of walking, depending on pace. With some 330 m of climbing, the loop is distinctly hilly, with about half the distance away from tarmac.

On small paths, the loop links the prettiest corners around the village. The circuit carries the blue Auto-Pédestre waymark throughout; being a loop, it has no fixed start.
