Around Dudelange Ginzebierg the Minett — Luxembourg's south — shows its green side: forests and species-rich heath have reclaimed the former mining land. At 10.4 km, the circuit takes roughly 3 hours. The route gathers about 150 m of ascent, and most of the way follows forest and field tracks.

On small paths, the loop links the prettiest corners around the village. Like every Auto-Pédestre, the loop is permanently waymarked with the blue symbol and can be started at any point.
