This Auto-Pédestre explores the surroundings of Mondercange in the Guttland, the varied heart of the country between fields and woods. The route measures 7.2 km — about 2 hours of walking, depending on pace. There is barely any climbing, with about half the distance away from tarmac.

On the route: Wolfswiss. The circuit carries the blue Auto-Pédestre waymark throughout; being a loop, it has no fixed start.
