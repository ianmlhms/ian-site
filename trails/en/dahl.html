This Auto-Pédestre explores the surroundings of Dahl in the Éislek, Luxembourg's hilly north of quiet forests and deeply cut river valleys. At 8.7 km, the circuit takes roughly 2½ hours. With some 300 m of climbing, the loop is distinctly hilly, and most of the way follows forest and field tracks.

On the route: Hide Out FEB/JUN 1944. Like every Auto-Pédestre, the loop is permanently waymarked with the blue symbol and can be started at any point.
