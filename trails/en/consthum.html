This Auto-Pédestre explores the surroundings of Consthum in the Éislek, Luxembourg's hilly north of quiet forests and deeply cut river valleys. The loop is 7.8 km long; at a normal pace expect about 2½ hours. The route gathers about 190 m of ascent, and most of the way follows forest and field tracks.

On the route, among others: Schüttbuerg and Geesselee. The circuit carries the blue Auto-Pédestre waymark throughout; being a loop, it has no fixed start.
