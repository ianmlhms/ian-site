Around Strassen, in central Luxembourg, you cross the Guttland: gentle hills, fields, stream valleys and stretches of forest. The route measures 8.2 km — about 2 hours of walking, depending on pace. There is barely any climbing, and most of the way follows forest and field tracks.

Field and forest tracks alternate with quiet back roads. The circuit carries the blue Auto-Pédestre waymark throughout; being a loop, it has no fixed start.
