Hesperange lies in the Guttland — quiet farm tracks, woodland passages and village edges give this loop its rhythm. At 4.3 km, the circuit takes roughly 1 hours. There is barely any climbing, with about half the distance away from tarmac.

Along the way you pass Buerg Hesper. The circuit carries the blue Auto-Pédestre waymark throughout; being a loop, it has no fixed start.
