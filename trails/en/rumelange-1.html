Rumelange is part of the Minett in the south of the country — the loop combines woodland, open country and echoes of the region's industrial past. At 8.9 km, the circuit takes roughly 2½ hours. The route gathers about 130 m of ascent, with about half the distance away from tarmac.

Worth a look en route: Polverhaischen and Musée national des Mines. Like every Auto-Pédestre, the loop is permanently waymarked with the blue symbol and can be started at any point.
