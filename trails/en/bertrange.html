Around Bertrange, in central Luxembourg, you cross the Guttland: gentle hills, fields, stream valleys and stretches of forest. The loop is 7.3 km long; at a normal pace expect about 2 hours. There is barely any climbing, and most of the way follows forest and field tracks.

On the route, among others: Zeiwelt and Ënneschte Bësch. The circuit carries the blue Auto-Pédestre waymark throughout; being a loop, it has no fixed start.
