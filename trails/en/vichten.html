This Auto-Pédestre explores the surroundings of Vichten in the Guttland, the varied heart of the country between fields and woods. The loop is 11.7 km long; at a normal pace expect about 3 hours. The route gathers about 170 m of ascent, and most of the way follows forest and field tracks.

On the route: replica Roman mozaic Vichten. The circuit carries the blue Auto-Pédestre waymark throughout; being a loop, it has no fixed start.
