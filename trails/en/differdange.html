Around Differdange the Minett — Luxembourg's south — shows its green side: forests and species-rich heath have reclaimed the former mining land. At 10.7 km, the circuit takes roughly 3 hours. The route gathers about 200 m of ascent, and most of the way follows forest and field tracks.

Along the way you pass Espace Muséologique and Musée Eugène Pesch, among other sights. The circuit carries the blue Auto-Pédestre waymark throughout; being a loop, it has no fixed start.
