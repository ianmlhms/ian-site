Around Stadtbredimus, vineyards and views over the Moselle valley set the scene — the loop links vine slopes, plateau tracks and quiet side valleys. At 12.2 km, the circuit takes roughly 3½ hours. The route gathers about 220 m of ascent, with about half the distance away from tarmac.

Worth a look en route: Château de Stradtbredimus and Intepéilchen. Like every Auto-Pédestre, the loop is permanently waymarked with the blue symbol and can be started at any point.
