Mensdorf lies in the Guttland — quiet farm tracks, woodland passages and village edges give this loop its rhythm. At 10.3 km, the circuit takes roughly 3 hours. The route gathers about 170 m of ascent, and most of the way follows forest and field tracks.

The route mostly follows near-natural paths through woods and open country. The circuit carries the blue Auto-Pédestre waymark throughout; being a loop, it has no fixed start.
