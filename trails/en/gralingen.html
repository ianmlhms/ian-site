Gralingen sits in the heart of the Éislek — the loop crosses the mix of ridgelines, forest and open pasture typical of the country's north. The route measures 9.6 km — about 2½ hours of walking, depending on pace. The route gathers about 200 m of ascent, and most of the way follows forest and field tracks.

On small paths, the loop links the prettiest corners around the village. Like every Auto-Pédestre, the loop is permanently waymarked with the blue symbol and can be started at any point.
