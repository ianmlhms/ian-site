This Auto-Pédestre explores the surroundings of Schuttrange in the Guttland, the varied heart of the country between fields and woods. The loop is 4.8 km long; at a normal pace expect about 1½ hours. There is barely any climbing, and most of the way follows forest and field tracks.

The route mostly follows near-natural paths through woods and open country. The circuit carries the blue Auto-Pédestre waymark throughout; being a loop, it has no fixed start.
