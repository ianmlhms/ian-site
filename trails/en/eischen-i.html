Around Eischen, in central Luxembourg, you cross the Guttland: gentle hills, fields, stream valleys and stretches of forest. The route measures 7.7 km — about 2½ hours of walking, depending on pace. The route gathers about 250 m of ascent, and most of the way follows forest and field tracks.

On small paths, the loop links the prettiest corners around the village. Like every Auto-Pédestre, the loop is permanently waymarked with the blue symbol and can be started at any point.
