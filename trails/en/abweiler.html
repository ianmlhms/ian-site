Around Abweiler, in central Luxembourg, you cross the Guttland: gentle hills, fields, stream valleys and stretches of forest. The loop is 8.9 km long; at a normal pace expect about 2½ hours. There is barely any climbing, and most of the way follows forest and field tracks.

On the route: Beetebuerger Bësch. The circuit carries the blue Auto-Pédestre waymark throughout; being a loop, it has no fixed start.
