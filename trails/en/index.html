Luxembourg's Auto-Pédestre loop trails

The Auto-Pédestres are Luxembourg's classic circular walks: more than 100 local loops across the country, permanently waymarked with the blue symbol and always bringing you back to where you started. Many were laid out decades ago for day-trippers arriving by car — hence the name — but today most are easy to reach by bus, since public transport is free throughout Luxembourg.

This site presents the trails one by one — with an interactive map, distance, approximate walking time, difficulty and the bus stops right on the route. Route data comes from OpenStreetMap, stop data from Luxembourg's official timetable feed.
