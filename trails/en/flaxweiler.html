This Auto-Pédestre tours the surroundings of Flaxweiler on the Luxembourg Moselle, between vine rows, orchards and glimpses across to the German bank. At 12.7 km, the circuit takes roughly 3½ hours. The route gathers about 220 m of ascent, and most of the way follows forest and field tracks.

Field and forest tracks alternate with quiet back roads. The circuit carries the blue Auto-Pédestre waymark throughout; being a loop, it has no fixed start.
