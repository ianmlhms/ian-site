This Auto-Pédestre explores the surroundings of Lorentzweiler in the Guttland, the varied heart of the country between fields and woods. The route measures 9.1 km — about 2½ hours of walking, depending on pace. The route gathers about 260 m of ascent, with about half the distance away from tarmac.

On the route, among others: Fautefiels and Maison néolithique. The circuit carries the blue Auto-Pédestre waymark throughout; being a loop, it has no fixed start.
