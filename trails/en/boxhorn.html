This Auto-Pédestre explores the surroundings of Boxhorn in the Éislek, Luxembourg's hilly north of quiet forests and deeply cut river valleys. The loop is 12 km long; at a normal pace expect about 3½ hours. The route gathers about 180 m of ascent, and most of the way follows forest and field tracks.

Along the way you pass Museum Moulin d'Asselborn. The circuit carries the blue Auto-Pédestre waymark throughout; being a loop, it has no fixed start.
