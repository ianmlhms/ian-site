Around Garnich, in central Luxembourg, you cross the Guttland: gentle hills, fields, stream valleys and stretches of forest. At 8.8 km, the circuit takes roughly 2½ hours. The route gathers about 120 m of ascent, with about half the distance away from tarmac.

Field and forest tracks alternate with quiet back roads. Like every Auto-Pédestre, the loop is permanently waymarked with the blue symbol and can be started at any point.
