This Auto-Pédestre explores the surroundings of Dalheim in the Guttland, the varied heart of the country between fields and woods. The loop is 7.9 km long; at a normal pace expect about 2 hours. There is barely any climbing, and most of the way follows forest and field tracks.

On small paths, the loop links the prettiest corners around the village. The circuit carries the blue Auto-Pédestre waymark throughout; being a loop, it has no fixed start.
