This Auto-Pédestre explores the surroundings of Redange in the Guttland, the varied heart of the country between fields and woods. The route measures 8 km — about 2 hours of walking, depending on pace. The route gathers about 120 m of ascent, with about half the distance away from tarmac.

On small paths, the loop links the prettiest corners around the village. Like every Auto-Pédestre, the loop is permanently waymarked with the blue symbol and can be started at any point.
