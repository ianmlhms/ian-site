This Auto-Pédestre explores the surroundings of Steinsel in the Guttland, the varied heart of the country between fields and woods. At 9.6 km, the circuit takes roughly 3 hours. The route gathers about 280 m of ascent, with about half the distance away from tarmac.

On small paths, the loop links the prettiest corners around the village. The circuit carries the blue Auto-Pédestre waymark throughout; being a loop, it has no fixed start.
