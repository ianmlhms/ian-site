This Auto-Pédestre tours the surroundings of Wormeldange on the Luxembourg Moselle, between vine rows, orchards and glimpses across to the German bank. The route measures 5.1 km — about 1½ hours of walking, depending on pace. There is barely any climbing, and most of the way follows forest and field tracks.

The route mostly follows near-natural paths through woods and open country. The circuit carries the blue Auto-Pédestre waymark throughout; being a loop, it has no fixed start.
