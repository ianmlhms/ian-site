Around Steinfort, in central Luxembourg, you cross the Guttland: gentle hills, fields, stream valleys and stretches of forest. The route measures 9.4 km — about 2½ hours of walking, depending on pace. There is barely any climbing, and most of the way follows forest and field tracks.

Along the way you pass Steekollen. Like every Auto-Pédestre, the loop is permanently waymarked with the blue symbol and can be started at any point.
