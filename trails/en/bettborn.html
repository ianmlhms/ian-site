Around Bettborn, in central Luxembourg, you cross the Guttland: gentle hills, fields, stream valleys and stretches of forest. The route measures 10 km — about 3 hours of walking, depending on pace. The route gathers about 180 m of ascent, and most of the way follows forest and field tracks.

The route mostly follows near-natural paths through woods and open country. The circuit carries the blue Auto-Pédestre waymark throughout; being a loop, it has no fixed start.
