Around Boulaide the Éislek shows its classic face: deep valleys, wooded ridges and wide views over Luxembourg's Ardennes. At 7 km, the circuit takes roughly 2 hours. The route gathers about 180 m of ascent, with about half the distance away from tarmac.

On the route: Houfels. The circuit carries the blue Auto-Pédestre waymark throughout; being a loop, it has no fixed start.
