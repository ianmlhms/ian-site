This Auto-Pédestre explores the surroundings of Schlindermanderscheid in the Éislek, Luxembourg's hilly north of quiet forests and deeply cut river valleys. The route measures 7.5 km — about 2½ hours of walking, depending on pace. The route gathers about 270 m of ascent, and most of the way follows forest and field tracks.

The route mostly follows near-natural paths through woods and open country. The circuit carries the blue Auto-Pédestre waymark throughout; being a loop, it has no fixed start.
