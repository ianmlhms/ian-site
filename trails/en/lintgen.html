This Auto-Pédestre explores the surroundings of Lintgen in the Guttland, the varied heart of the country between fields and woods. The loop is 12.3 km long; at a normal pace expect about 3½ hours. The route gathers about 260 m of ascent, and most of the way follows forest and field tracks.

On small paths, the loop links the prettiest corners around the village. The circuit carries the blue Auto-Pédestre waymark throughout; being a loop, it has no fixed start.
