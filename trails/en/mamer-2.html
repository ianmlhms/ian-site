This Auto-Pédestre explores the surroundings of Mamer in the Guttland, the varied heart of the country between fields and woods. The route measures 6.4 km — about 1½ hours of walking, depending on pace. There is barely any climbing, and most of the way follows forest and field tracks.

On the route: Direbond. The circuit carries the blue Auto-Pédestre waymark throughout; being a loop, it has no fixed start.
