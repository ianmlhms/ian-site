Around Belvaux the Minett — Luxembourg's south — shows its green side: forests and species-rich heath have reclaimed the former mining land. At 9.4 km, the circuit takes roughly 2½ hours. The route gathers about 170 m of ascent, with about half the distance away from tarmac.

On the route: Zolwerknapp. The circuit carries the blue Auto-Pédestre waymark throughout; being a loop, it has no fixed start.
