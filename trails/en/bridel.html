This Auto-Pédestre explores the surroundings of Bridel in the Guttland, the varied heart of the country between fields and woods. The route measures 8.2 km — about 2 hours of walking, depending on pace. There is barely any climbing, with about half the distance away from tarmac.

Along the way you pass Missiounskraiz. The circuit carries the blue Auto-Pédestre waymark throughout; being a loop, it has no fixed start.
