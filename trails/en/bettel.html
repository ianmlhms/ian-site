This Auto-Pédestre explores the surroundings of Bettel in the Éislek, Luxembourg's hilly north of quiet forests and deeply cut river valleys. At 8.8 km, the circuit takes roughly 2½ hours. The route gathers about 190 m of ascent, with about half the distance away from tarmac.

On the route: Panorama pavillon. The circuit carries the blue Auto-Pédestre waymark throughout; being a loop, it has no fixed start.
