This Auto-Pédestre explores the surroundings of Dippach in the Guttland, the varied heart of the country between fields and woods. The route measures 9.1 km — about 2½ hours of walking, depending on pace. There is barely any climbing, and most of the way follows forest and field tracks.

The route mostly follows near-natural paths through woods and open country. The circuit carries the blue Auto-Pédestre waymark throughout; being a loop, it has no fixed start.
