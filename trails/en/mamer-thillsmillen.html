Mamer Thillsmillen lies in the Guttland — quiet farm tracks, woodland passages and village edges give this loop its rhythm. The route measures 7.7 km — about 2 hours of walking, depending on pace. There is barely any climbing, and most of the way follows forest and field tracks.

Along the way you pass Réserve naturelle communale Driesch. Like every Auto-Pédestre, the loop is permanently waymarked with the blue symbol and can be started at any point.
