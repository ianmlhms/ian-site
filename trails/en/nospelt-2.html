Around Nospelt, in central Luxembourg, you cross the Guttland: gentle hills, fields, stream valleys and stretches of forest. The loop is 6.9 km long; at a normal pace expect about 2 hours. The route gathers about 120 m of ascent, with about half the distance away from tarmac.

On the route, among others: Ausgriewermusee and Musée de la Poterie. Like every Auto-Pédestre, the loop is permanently waymarked with the blue symbol and can be started at any point.
