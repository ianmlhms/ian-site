This Auto-Pédestre explores the surroundings of Nospelt in the Guttland, the varied heart of the country between fields and woods. The route measures 7.6 km — about 2 hours of walking, depending on pace. There is barely any climbing, and most of the way follows forest and field tracks.

Worth a look en route: Ausgriewermusee and Musée de la Poterie. The circuit carries the blue Auto-Pédestre waymark throughout; being a loop, it has no fixed start.
