Wiltz sits in the heart of the Éislek — the loop links the forest paths, gravel roads and open ridge sections typical of the north. At 10.5 km, the tour takes roughly 1½ hours to ride. There are no big climbs — the loop rolls fast, mixing quiet lanes with forest and field tracks.

Worth a look en route: Sanatorium Kaul, foundation ruins (1920s) and Pompjeesmusee. The tour is signposted as a loop and can be started anywhere — helmet on and off you go.
