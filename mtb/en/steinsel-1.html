Route line from OpenStreetMap — small deviations on the ground are possible. The MTB signposts on site are authoritative.

Around Steinsel, in the Guttland, the tour rolls over farm tracks, forest sections and quiet lanes — ideal terrain for covering distance. The route measures 0.4 km — about ½ hours of riding, depending on pace. There are no big climbs — the loop rolls fast, and most of it runs off-tarmac on forest and field tracks.

On small tracks, the tour links the prettiest corners of the area. The tour is signposted as a loop and can be started anywhere — helmet on and off you go.
