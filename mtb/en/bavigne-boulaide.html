This MTB loop explores the area around Bavigne - Boulaide in the Éislek, where deep valleys and wooded ridgelines keep the trail constantly rising and falling. At 37.1 km, the tour takes roughly 4 hours to ride. The route gathers about 370 m of climbing, and most of it runs off-tarmac on forest and field tracks.

Along the way you pass Tempelskamp. The tour is signposted as a loop and can be started anywhere — helmet on and off you go.
