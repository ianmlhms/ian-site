This MTB loop explores the area around Bech in the Mullerthal region, with sandstone cliffs and dense forest as the backdrop. The loop is 1.6 km long; at a normal pace expect about ½ hours in the saddle. There are no big climbs — the loop rolls fast, and most of it runs off-tarmac on forest and field tracks.

On small tracks, the tour links the prettiest corners of the area. As a waymarked loop the route has no fixed start; ride in the direction of the arrows.
