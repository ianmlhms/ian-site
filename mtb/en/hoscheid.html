Hoscheid sits in the heart of the Éislek — the loop links the forest paths, gravel roads and open ridge sections typical of the north. The route measures 24.8 km — about 3 hours of riding, depending on pace. The route gathers about 460 m of climbing, with about half the distance unpaved.

Along the way you pass Hockslay and Bomm, among other sights. As a waymarked loop the route has no fixed start; ride in the direction of the arrows.
