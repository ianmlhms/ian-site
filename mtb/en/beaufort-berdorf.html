This MTB loop explores the area around Beaufort-Berdorf in the Éislek, where deep valleys and wooded ridgelines keep the trail constantly rising and falling. The route measures 38.5 km — about 4½ hours of riding, depending on pace. With some 570 m of climbing, the tour demands fitness, and most of it runs off-tarmac on forest and field tracks.

Along the way you pass Buerg Beefort and Siewenschlueff, among other sights. The tour is signposted as a loop and can be started anywhere — helmet on and off you go.
