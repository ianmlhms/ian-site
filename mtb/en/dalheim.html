This MTB loop explores the surroundings of Dalheim in the Guttland, the rolling centre of the country between fields and woods. The loop is 23.3 km long; at a normal pace expect about 2½ hours in the saddle. There are no big climbs — the loop rolls fast, with about half the distance unpaved.

On small tracks, the tour links the prettiest corners of the area. As a waymarked loop the route has no fixed start; ride in the direction of the arrows.
