Route line from OpenStreetMap — small deviations on the ground are possible. The MTB signposts on site are authoritative.

Steinsel lies in the Guttland — the loop alternates between rolling farmland sections and short stretches of forest. The loop is 0.5 km long; at a normal pace expect about ½ hours in the saddle. There are no big climbs — the loop rolls fast, and most of it runs off-tarmac on forest and field tracks.

On small tracks, the tour links the prettiest corners of the area. As a waymarked loop the route has no fixed start; ride in the direction of the arrows.
