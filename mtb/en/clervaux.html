Clervaux sits in the heart of the Éislek — the loop links the forest paths, gravel roads and open ridge sections typical of the north. The loop is 24.8 km long; at a normal pace expect about 3 hours in the saddle. The route gathers about 390 m of climbing, with about half the distance unpaved.

Worth a look en route: Villa Berlaymont and Château de Clervaux. As a waymarked loop the route has no fixed start; ride in the direction of the arrows.
