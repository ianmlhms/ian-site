Hosingen sits in the heart of the Éislek — the loop links the forest paths, gravel roads and open ridge sections typical of the north. The loop is 12.7 km long; at a normal pace expect about 1½ hours in the saddle. The route gathers about 240 m of climbing, and most of it runs off-tarmac on forest and field tracks.

Most of the riding is on natural-surface tracks through woods and open country. The tour is signposted as a loop and can be started anywhere — helmet on and off you go.
