Around Wiltz the tour crosses the Éislek, Luxembourg's mountainous north — long climbs, fast forest descents and wide views over the Ardennes ridges. At 25.2 km, the tour takes roughly 3 hours to ride. The route gathers about 370 m of climbing, with about half the distance unpaved.

Worth a look en route: Château de Wiltz and National Tannery Museum. As a waymarked loop the route has no fixed start; ride in the direction of the arrows.
