Around Hosingen - Lellingen the tour crosses the Éislek, Luxembourg's mountainous north — long climbs, fast forest descents and wide views over the Ardennes ridges. The route measures 21.4 km — about 2½ hours of riding, depending on pace. The route gathers about 300 m of climbing, with about half the distance unpaved.

Along the way you pass Vue Kiischpelt and Geesselee, among other sights. The tour is signposted as a loop and can be started anywhere — helmet on and off you go.
