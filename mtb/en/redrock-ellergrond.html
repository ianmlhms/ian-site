Around Esch-sur-Alzette you ride straight through the Minett: former open-cast mines, red earth and a surprisingly intricate network of trails and forest roads. The loop is 28.9 km long; at a normal pace expect about 3 hours in the saddle. The route gathers about 250 m of climbing, and most of it runs off-tarmac on forest and field tracks.

On the route, among others: Polverhaischen and Grouwen a Feldbunn. As a waymarked loop the route has no fixed start; ride in the direction of the arrows.
