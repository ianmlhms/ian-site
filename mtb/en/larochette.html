Larochette lies in the Guttland — the loop alternates between rolling farmland sections and short stretches of forest. The route measures 26.7 km — about 3 hours of riding, depending on pace. The route gathers about 270 m of climbing, and most of it runs off-tarmac on forest and field tracks.

On the route, among others: Château de Meysembourg and Verluerekascht. As a waymarked loop the route has no fixed start; ride in the direction of the arrows.
