Saeul lies in the Guttland — the loop alternates between rolling farmland sections and short stretches of forest. The loop is 7.9 km long; at a normal pace expect about 1 hours in the saddle. There are no big climbs — the loop rolls fast, and most of it runs off-tarmac on forest and field tracks.

Forest and field tracks alternate with quiet back roads. The tour is signposted as a loop and can be started anywhere — helmet on and off you go.
