Route line from OpenStreetMap — small deviations on the ground are possible. The MTB signposts on site are authoritative.

Kayl is part of the Minett — short steep ramps, fast corners and industrial heritage beside the trail define the loop. The route measures 32.7 km — about 3½ hours of riding, depending on pace. The route gathers about 250 m of climbing, and most of it runs off-tarmac on forest and field tracks.

Worth a look en route: Eisenerzgrube and Plaakesch Kopp. As a waymarked loop the route has no fixed start; ride in the direction of the arrows.
