Route line from OpenStreetMap — small deviations on the ground are possible. The MTB signposts on site are authoritative.

Around Leudelange, in the Guttland, the tour rolls over farm tracks, forest sections and quiet lanes — ideal terrain for covering distance. At 1.1 km, the tour takes roughly ½ hours to ride. There are no big climbs — the loop rolls fast, mixing quiet lanes with forest and field tracks.

Most of the riding is on natural-surface tracks through woods and open country. The tour is signposted as a loop and can be started anywhere — helmet on and off you go.
