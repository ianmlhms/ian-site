This MTB loop explores the surroundings of Wormeldange on the Moselle: vines, plateau farm tracks and descents back towards the river. The route measures 24.2 km — about 2½ hours of riding, depending on pace. The route gathers about 270 m of climbing, and most of it runs off-tarmac on forest and field tracks.

Most of the riding is on natural-surface tracks through woods and open country. As a waymarked loop the route has no fixed start; ride in the direction of the arrows.

Highlights
