Ingeldorf-Ettelbrück sits in the heart of the Éislek — the loop links the forest paths, gravel roads and open ridge sections typical of the north. At 20.1 km, the tour takes roughly 3 hours to ride. With some 520 m of climbing, the tour demands fitness, and most of it runs off-tarmac on forest and field tracks.

Along the way you pass General Patton Memorial and Ditgesbaach, among other sights. The tour is signposted as a loop and can be started anywhere — helmet on and off you go.
