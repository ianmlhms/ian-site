This MTB loop explores the area around Differdange in the Red Rocks region, where the old mining land now hides some of the country's best trails. The route measures 28.5 km — about 3 hours of riding, depending on pace. There are no big climbs — the loop rolls fast, and most of it runs off-tarmac on forest and field tracks.

Worth a look en route: Rocky Spot and Mine Grouwen. The tour is signposted as a loop and can be started anywhere — helmet on and off you go.
