This MTB loop explores the surroundings of Weiler-la-Tour in the Guttland, the rolling centre of the country between fields and woods. The route measures 21.3 km — about 2½ hours of riding, depending on pace. There are no big climbs — the loop rolls fast, with about half the distance unpaved.

Along the way you pass Château d'Aspelt. The tour is signposted as a loop and can be started anywhere — helmet on and off you go.
