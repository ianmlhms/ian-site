Route line from OpenStreetMap — small deviations on the ground are possible. The MTB signposts on site are authoritative.

Saeul lies in the Guttland — the loop alternates between rolling farmland sections and short stretches of forest. The route measures 18.6 km — about 2 hours of riding, depending on pace. The route gathers about 210 m of climbing, and most of it runs off-tarmac on forest and field tracks.

Forest and field tracks alternate with quiet back roads. The tour is signposted as a loop and can be started anywhere — helmet on and off you go.
